# Code of Conduct

Handoff Forge is committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, specific, and constructive.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence and curiosity.
- Respect different experience levels and help newcomers succeed.
- Protect the privacy of source material, diagnostics, and contributor information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing another person's private information, or deliberately weakening the project's safety boundaries are not acceptable. Do not use project channels to expose credentials or confidential documents.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close contributions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and applied consistently.

Use GitHub's reporting tools for the relevant content or account. If project maintainer action is
also needed, open a minimal issue asking to establish private contact, without including the conduct
details. Do not post sensitive conduct reports in a public issue.
